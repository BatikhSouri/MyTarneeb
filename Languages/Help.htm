Rules of the game of Tarneeb :

 

  • The game of Tarneeb comes from the arab world. It is still widely played. This game is mainly played in the Middle East.
  • The game is played by 4 players. These are split into two teams of two players.
  • Each player sits in front of his team mate
  • A deck of 52 cards is used
  • The card with the highest value is the ace, and the one with the lowest value is the 2.
  • The team of the player who has played the highest card during a round wins a point.
  • The Tarneeb is a family of cards of higher value, chosen by the player who won the bet in the beginning of the game.
  • In a game, there are three stages: the bet, the game itself and the points counting at the end.

 

The bet :

After the cards distribution, each player bets the number of rounds his team is going to win. The bet must be between 7 and 13. A player has to bet more than has been said before, otherwise he passes his turn. When the players have bet what they can, the winner chooses the Tarneeb family. It is generally the family of which that player has the most of the cards.

 

The game :

The player who won the bet begins the game. In the following rounds, the player who begins is the one who the last round, which means, the one who have the card with the highest value. During, the players have the obligation to play a card of the same family of the first card if the can. Otherwise, they can play a card of any family (Tarneeb or not). A player who puts a card of an other family than the first card doesn’t win the round, unless if it is a card of the Tarneeb family.

 

Points counting ;

Each round won by a team gives it a point. If the team who won the bet at the beginning of the game reaches its objective she won the game. Otherwise, the other team won the game.
